Common questions

Everything you need to know

How accurate is the cost estimate?

Our estimates are based on live exchange rates, standard air freight rates, and Tanzania's published import duty bands. The final price may vary by ±5–10% depending on exact package weight and customs assessment. You are always told the confirmed total before you pay.

How do I pay?

We accept M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, and bank transfer (CRDB, NMB). Payment is split: a deposit when we confirm your order, and the balance on delivery. No foreign credit card needed.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders from the US and UK arrive in 7–14 business days. China and Asia take 10–20 days. We offer express air shipping for time-sensitive orders — ask us on WhatsApp for a quote.

What if my item is out of stock?

We check availability before confirming your order. If an item sells out after we place it, we'll find you an equivalent alternative or refund you in full — no deductions, no hassle.

Are there items you can't bring in?

We can't import items prohibited by Tanzania Customs Authority — including certain electronics, medications, perishables, and hazardous materials. We'll tell you if your item falls into a restricted category before accepting your order.

Can I track my order?

Yes. You receive a JustBuy tracking link and WhatsApp updates at every key stage: purchased, dispatched, in transit, arrived in Tanzania, cleared customs, and out for delivery.
